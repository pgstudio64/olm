4. Distance display rule (V-01)

An inter-block distance is displayed if and only if:
1. One is the nearest neighbour of the other in a cardinal direction (right or down).
2. The desk footprints (physical, without zones) of both blocks overlap on the perpendicular axis: EW overlap for vertical neighbours, NS overlap for horizontal neighbours.

Circulation/clearance zones do not participate in the overlap test.
